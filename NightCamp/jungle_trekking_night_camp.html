Wilderness Protocol

Safety First

  • Stay with your pack: Never wander off the trekking paths alone.
  • Hydration is vital: Carry and consume sufficient water during the trek.
  • Obey the guides: Follow instructor directions immediately and without question.
  • Leave no trace: Respect wildlife and ensure the campsite is cleaner than we found it.
  • Fire & Blades: Use knives and campfires responsibly. Supervise fires at all times.
